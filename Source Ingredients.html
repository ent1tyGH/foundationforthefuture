Suppliers

OIKOS PH

Oikos Philippines is a local supplier dedicated to providing creative and environmentally friendly packaging solutions. The company offers eco-friendly alternatives to traditional paper and plastic bags, including biodegradable bags manufactured from cassava and corn starch. Oikos Philippines aspires to lead greener, more sustainable packaging solutions.

Natura Aeropack

Natura Aeropack provides innovative, sustainable packaging solutions for personal care and beauty brands. With a strong commitment to reducing environmental impact, they help companies transition to greener, more responsible packaging practices.
