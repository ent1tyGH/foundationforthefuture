Partners

Here are the incredible organizations, brands, and companies who share our commitment to ethical practices and have helped turn our vision into reality.

CCIP

A non-profit organization of distributors, traders, manufacturers, raw materials suppliers, packaging, labeling and equipment suppliers, and allied services in the cosmetics industry.

PARMS

The Philippine Alliance for Recycling and Materials Sustainability (PARMS) is a non-profit organization in the Philippines focused on developing sustainable waste management solutions. PARMS envisions a society in which sustainability is ingrained at every stage of production and consumption, paving the way for a cleaner, more waste-resilient future for the nation.

BAN TOXICS

BAN Toxics is a non-governmental organization dedicated to building a toxic-free society in which people and the environment can thrive. By leading advocacy initiatives, encouraging effective chemical management, and empowering communities, the organization helps to create safer, healthier, and more environmentally friendly environments for future generations.

PILI ANI

Pili Ani is a proudly Filipino beauty and wellness brand that celebrates the power of the pili tree. By combining traditional knowledge with modern skincare innovation, they create products that nourish the skin while supporting local farmers and sustainable sourcing.

PURE CULTURE

Pure Culture champions conscious beauty with clean, cruelty-free skincare that respects both people and the planet. Their products are thoughtfully formulated to meet the needs of modern consumers who care about what goes on their skin and the impact it leaves behind.

GRWM COSMETICS

GRWM Cosmetics is redefining what it means to be a beauty brand in the Philippines. Beyond offering inclusive, high‑performance products, they are actively embedding sustainability and social good into their mission.

OIKOS PH

Oikos Philippines is a local supplier dedicated to providing creative and environmentally friendly packaging solutions. The company offers eco-friendly alternatives to traditional paper and plastic bags, including biodegradable bags manufactured from cassava and corn starch. Oikos Philippines aspires to lead greener, more sustainable packaging solutions.

Natura Aeropack

Natura Aeropack provides innovative, sustainable packaging solutions for personal care and beauty brands. With a strong commitment to reducing environmental impact, they help companies transition to greener, more responsible packaging practices.

Bioskin Philippines

Is a locally-made skincare company in the Philippines that is focused on natural, eco-friendly, cruelty-free, and locally sourced products.
