FOUNDATION FOR THE FUTURE

Our Story

We are Multimedia Arts students from De La Salle College of Saint Benilde who are motivated by
the desire to make beauty more sustainable. 

What began as a classroom concept has evolved into a mission to drive change in the cosmetics industry.
We want to be a voice that encourages local brands to think green, act responsibly, and redefine beauty.

We believe that with our efforts, even as students, we are able to spark and bring about change.

Foundation for the Future's

Vision-Mission

Vision

We envision a future where the beauty industry is fully sustainable, honest, and driven by purpose. A world where entrepreneurs and consumers alike prioritize the well-being of the planet and the people, inspiring not only local but global communities to redefine beauty as a force to inspire positive change and redefine what it means to truly care.

Mission

We are dedicated to driving change in the cosmetic industry by creating a more sustainable and impactful beauty industry. Our mission is to empower aspiring beauty entrepreneurs to delve into the real environmental effects of cosmetics, identify greenwashing, and make informed choices that truly care for both people and the planet. Our goal is to build a friendly, collaborative space where founders can learn, share ideas, and grow together, proving that beauty goes beyond looks and has the power to create real, meaningful change.
